This section lists user stories that are specific to the use case of founding a new "Besloten Vennootschap" (BV), a Dutch private limited liability company (Ltd.). Most of these user stories can be handled by the general user stories defined above, but are to show the general requirements cover the use case specific requirements as well.

### F.1 Receive Incorporation Deed Attestation from Notary

As an Authorized Representative I want to receive an ["Incorporation Deed"](/functional/use-cases/founding-a-company#incorporation-deed) Attestation from a Notary on behalf of the Legal Entity in the Organisational Wallet.

### F.2 Present Incorporation Deed Attestation to Chamber of Commerce

As an Authorized Representative I want to present an ["Incorporation Deed"](/functional/use-cases/founding-a-company#incorporation-deed) Attestation stored in the Organisational Wallet to the Dutch Chamber of Commerce (KvK).

### F.3 Receive Chamber of Commerce Number Attestation from Chamber of Commerce

As an Authorized Representative I want to receive an ["Chamber of Commerce Number"](/functional/use-cases/founding-a-company#chamber-of-commerce-number) Attestation from the Dutch Chamber of Commerce (KvK) on behalf of the Legal Entity in the Organisational Wallet.

### F.4 Receive RSIN Attestation from Chamber of Commerce

As an Authorized Representative I want to receive an ["RSIN"](/functional/use-cases/founding-a-company#rsin) Attestation from the Dutch Chamber of Commerce (KvK) on behalf of the Legal Entity in the Organisational Wallet.

### F.5 Present Incorpoartion Deed Attestation to Tax Authority

As an Authorized Representative I want to present an ["Incorporation Deed"](/functional/use-cases/founding-a-company#incorporation-deed) Attestation stored in the Organisational Wallet on behalf of the Legal Entity to the Dutch Tax Authority (Belastingdienst).

### F.6 Receive VAT ID Attestation from Dutch Tax Authority

As an Authorized Representative I want to receive an ["VAT ID"](/functional/use-cases/founding-a-company#vat-id) Attestation from the Dutch Tax Authority (Belastingdienst) on behalf of the Legal Entity in the Organisational Wallet.

### F.7 Present Incorpoartion Deed to Dutch Bank

As an Authorized Representative I want to present an ["Incorporation Deed"](/functional/use-cases/founding-a-company#incorporation-deed) Attestation stored in the Organisational Wallet on behalf of the Legal Entity to a Dutch Bank.

### F.8 Receive Bank Account Number Attestation from Dutch Bank

As an Authorized Representative I want to receive an ["Bank Account Number"](/functional/use-cases/founding-a-company#bank-account-number) Attestation from a Dutch Bank on behalf of the Legal Entity in the Organisational Wallet.
